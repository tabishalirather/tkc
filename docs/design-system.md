# The Kashmir Co. Design System

## 1. Design Philosophy
- Build a premium modern commerce experience with high clarity and low noise.
- Keep sections editorial, product-led, and conversion-aware.
- Use restraint: fewer visual effects, stronger hierarchy, cleaner rhythm.
- Feel warm and natural through material-inspired tones and thoughtful imagery.

## 2. Color Palette
Core neutrals:
- Canvas: #f8f7f2
- Soft section surface: #efefed
- Card neutral: #eceae6
- Base white: #ffffff
- Text dark: #1f1f1d
- Text muted: #6b645b

Brand accents:
- Saffron accent: #C18B2A to #9A6E1C
- Walnut deep: #5C3A1E
- Forest muted: #415D27

Usage rules:
- 80-90% of UI should stay neutral.
- Accent color should highlight CTAs, links, and small emphasis only.
- Avoid large saturated color blocks except controlled promo strip moments.

## 3. Typography
Families:
- Sans: Manrope
- Serif display: Cormorant Garamond

Scale:
- Hero title: 40-64px equivalent responsive classes
- Section titles: 32-40px equivalent with serif
- Body: 14-18px equivalent, mostly sans
- Micro labels: 11-12px uppercase, medium weight, tracked

Rules:
- Keep headlines short and clear.
- Keep supporting copy to 1-3 lines where possible.
- Use uppercase micro-labels sparingly for section anchoring.

## 4. Spacing Rules
- Main section vertical spacing: py-14 on mobile, py-20 to py-24 on desktop.
- Internal card spacing: p-4 to p-6.
- Major content blocks align to shared container width.
- Preserve breathing space between heading, support text, and action.

## 5. Navbar Rules
Structure:
- Optional dismissible promo strip above nav.
- Main nav with logo left, category links center, utility right.

Behavior:
- Sticky with subtle backdrop blur.
- Thin bottom separator only.
- Desktop links compact and evenly spaced.
- Mobile menu as clean stacked list with generous touch targets.

Utility actions:
- Need help text link.
- Account icon.
- Search icon.
- Cart icon with count badge.

## 6. Footer Rules
- Dark charcoal background with muted text.
- Four utility columns: Help, Shop, Explore, About.
- Brand summary + social links in first area.
- Bottom legal row with understated closing note.
- Keep links lightweight and vertically balanced.

## 7. Hero Pattern
- One dominant full-bleed lifestyle/product image.
- Soft dark overlay for text readability.
- Strong single headline, short support line.
- One primary CTA and one secondary CTA.
- Minimal trust points under CTA row.

## 8. Merchandising Tile Pattern
- Asymmetric or 2x2 editorial image grid.
- Tile content anchored to lower area with subtle overlay.
- Include title, short subtext, and compact CTA hint.
- Keep tile corners small and premium, not playful.

## 9. Product Card Pattern
Must include:
- Product image
- Name
- Short descriptor (optional)
- Price + optional strike-through
- Optional subtle badge
- Add-to-cart action

Rules:
- Avoid crowded multi-badge stacks.
- Avoid heavy shadows.
- Use thin borders and slight hover emphasis.
- Keep actions clear and compact.

## 10. Trust Section Pattern
- Four evenly distributed trust pillars.
- Circular image or icon per pillar.
- Short heading + 2-3 line description.
- Subtle text link under each pillar.

## 11. Form/Input Style
- Light neutral surface.
- Thin border, subtle focus ring in saffron accent.
- Compact but comfortable input height.
- Keep form copy concise and confidence-driven.

## 12. Motion & Interaction
- Transitions: 150ms-300ms ease.
- Hover effects: slight scale, border emphasis, or text color shift.
- Avoid flashy/looping animations.
- Focus states always visible and accessible.

## 13. Copy Tone
- Confident, modern, and natural.
- Short and commercially clear.
- No exaggerated health claims.
- No long marketing paragraphs on homepage.

## 14. Responsive Principles
- Mobile-first spacing and hierarchy.
- Preserve hero impact on small screens.
- Keep nav, promo, newsletter, and footer readable without crowding.
- Stack tile grids and trust pillars cleanly on narrow viewports.

## 15. Reuse Notes
Apply this system to:
- Shop and category listing pages
- Product detail pages
- Cart and checkout
- Account and order pages

This system is inspired by premium minimalist commerce references (including Bellroy-level polish and structure) while remaining original to The Kashmir Co. brand identity, voice, and product context.
